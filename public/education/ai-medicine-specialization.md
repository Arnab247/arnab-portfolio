## Certification Overview

### Core Competencies Acquired
* **Medical Image Diagnosis:** Developed Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to accurately detect and classify diseases in 2D and 3D medical images (e.g., MRI, X-ray).
* **Medical Prognosis Models:** Constructed tree-based prognostic models to predict patient survival rates and disease progression based on structured clinical data.
* **AI Medical Treatment Applications:** Modeled complex treatment effects using randomized control trial data and extracted critical diagnoses directly from unstructured clinical reports using Natural Language Processing (NLP).
* **Clinical Evaluation Nuance:** Mastered the statistical rigor required for clinical AI accuracy, including navigating class imbalances, calculating exact intersection-over-union, and mapping survival curves.
