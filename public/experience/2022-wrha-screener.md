# Frontline Healthcare Screening & Triage

**Role:** Hospital Entrance Screener (Casual/On-Call)  
**Institution:** Winnipeg Regional Health Authority (WRHA)  
**Core Skills:** Public Health Protocol, Risk Assessment, Crisis Communication, Interdisciplinary Teamwork

## Experience Overview

Served as the primary point of contact and triage at a major hospital facility, operating within a high-stakes clinical environment. Responsible for evaluating patients, visitors, and hospital staff against strict public health guidelines to mitigate infection risks and maintain overall facility security.

## Key Contributions & Responsibilities

### 1. Public Health Protocol Enforcement
* **Infection Control:** Conducted rigorous COVID-19 and infectious disease screenings for all individuals entering the facility, ensuring strict adherence to evolving regional health mandates.
* **Risk Assessment:** Rapidly evaluated incoming personnel and patients to identify potential health risks, redirecting symptomatic individuals to appropriate isolated care pathways.

### 2. Crisis Communication & Security
* **Interdisciplinary Collaboration:** Coordinated in real-time with nursing staff, hospital security, and administrative teams to secure the facility against both epidemiological and physical threats.
* **De-escalation & Triage:** Managed high-stress interactions with the public, utilizing calm, authoritative communication to process visitors efficiently while handling non-compliant or potentially volatile situations.

### 3. Clinical Environment Exposure
* **Frontline Operations:** Gained direct, on-the-ground exposure to the daily operational workflows, patient intake processes, and emergency protocols of a large-scale healthcare institution.
